ABOUT US

Disrupting travel industry since 2012. Step by step.

GoTravels, started in 2014, is India’s biggest online D-I-Y holiday booking platform that empowers users to curate customised holidays. The unique itinerary building platform considers factors like a traveller’s interests, trip duration, and budget to deliver the brand promise of ‘Craft Your Holiday, Your Way’, completely online. The travel company works in liaison with leading tourism boards of destinations like Australia, Singapore, Iceland, Japan, Dubai, New Zealand, besides 1,200+ hospitality partners around the world.

Our Mission

At GoTravels, our mission is simple: to make travel accessible, enjoyable, and enriching for everyone. Whether you're a seasoned globetrotter or a first-time traveler, we aim to inspire your wanderlust and turn your travel visions into reality.

Our Vision

Vision is to become a leading force in the travel industry by inspiring people to explore the world with passion, purpose, and ease. We aspire to make travel an enriching and transformative experience for all, where every journey sparks discovery, fosters cultural connections, and creates lasting memories.

We aim to set a new standard in personalized travel, blending innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction to create seamless travel experiences that not only meet but exceed the expectations of every traveler.

Know Us

OUR SUCCESSFUL STORY

GoTravels was founded with a simple mission: to provide affordable, customized travel experiences. Starting as a small travel agency catering to local destinations, we quickly gained a reputation for delivering quality service.

2010

GoTravels embraced digital solutions, launching our first website to provide an easy-to-use platform for clients to browse packages and make bookings. We also introduced customized tours, allowing clients to create their dream vacations.

2014

Our hard work was recognized as we received several industry awards, including "Best Emerging Travel Agency." This gave us visibility and credibility, further fueling our growth in the highly competitive travel market.

2018

GoTravels embraced full-scale digital transformation with the launch of our mobile app, providing clients with easy access to bookings, travel itineraries, and personalized recommendations.

2022
2012

Recognizing the growing demand, we expanded our services to include international travel packages, starting with popular destinations across Southeast Asia. Our team grew, and we added expert travel planners to offer tailored itineraries.

2016

To meet diverse client interests, we introduced specialized packages focused on adventure tourism and luxury vacations. From trekking in the Himalayas to luxury beach resorts in the Maldives, our range of offerings expanded significantly.

2020

Despite the COVID-19 pandemic, VoyageVista adapted by focusing on domestic travel and offering flexible booking policies. We introduced virtual travel experiences, allowing customers to explore destinations remotely, keeping our business afloat during challenging times.

2024

Celebrating 14 years of success, GoTravels now stands as a leading travel agency known for exceptional service, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We continue to evolve, offering unforgettable experiences while staying committed to sustainable and responsible travel.
